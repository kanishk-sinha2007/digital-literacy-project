# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ DO's
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts and comments  
- Use strong privacy settings to protect your personal information  
- Think before posting anything online  
- Share useful and positive content (educational, achievements, skills)  
- Keep your profile clean and appropriate for academic or professional viewing  

---

## ❌ DON'Ts
- Do not share sensitive personal information (phone number, address, passwords)  
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content  
- Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying  
- Avoid spreading fake news or unverified information  
- Do not post anything that could harm your reputation or future career  

---

## 📌 Note
Your social media presence creates a digital footprint that can be seen by recruiters, professors, and peers. Always use it responsibly.
