# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## 🔐 General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts  
- Enable two factor authentication wherever possible  
- Do not share personal or sensitive information online  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown attachments  
- Always verify the authenticity of websites before entering details  
- Keep your devices and apps updated regularly  


## 💳 UPI & Financial Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone under any circumstances  
- Remember, you do NOT need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money  
- Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources  
- Do not install unknown apps or screen sharing apps suggested by strangers  


## ⚠️ Awareness & Responsible Usage
- Do not trust unknown calls claiming to be from banks or companies  
- Avoid using public wifi for financial transactions  
- Log out from accounts when using shared devices  
- Be cautious while downloading files from the internet  


## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930** (available 24x7)  


## 📌 Note
Being aware and cautious while using digital platforms can prevent most cybercrimes. Always think before you click or share information online.
