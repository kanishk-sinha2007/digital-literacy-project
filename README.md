## 📌 Project Overview
This Digital Literacy Project is part of the CSE0001 course and focuses on developing essential skills required to navigate the digital world effectively and responsibly.  

As a Student Digital Ambassador, this project aims to build awareness about digital tools, professional communication, online safety, and responsible internet usage.

---

## 📚 Module Summaries

### 🔹 Module 1: Digital Literacy Basics
This module introduced the concept of digital literacy and its importance in modern education and daily life. It covered topics such as safe internet practices, digital tools for students, and maintaining a responsible online presence.

---

### 🔹 Module 2: Digital Portfolio
This module focused on building a professional online identity. It included creating and managing profiles on platforms like GitHub and LinkedIn, which are essential for academic and career growth.

---

### 🔹 Module 3: Online Platforms & Collaboration
This module explored coding practice platforms and collaborative tools. It helped in understanding how to improve problem-solving skills and use tools like Google Forms for data collection and teamwork.

---

### 🔹 Module 4: Digital Communication & Etiquette
This module emphasized professional communication through emails and responsible behavior on social media. It highlighted the importance of tone, structure, and clarity in digital interactions.

---

### 🔹 Module 5: Cyber Safety & Awareness
This module focused on identifying cyber threats such as phishing and UPI fraud. It also covered preventive measures and reporting mechanisms to ensure safe online practices.
